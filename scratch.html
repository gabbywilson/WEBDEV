Show Summary

Criminal Minds is a show about an elite group of FBI profilers that work to solve cases involving some of the country's cruelest criminals. They are apart of the Behavioral Analysis Unit, also known as the BAU, which works to understand the mind of the criminal and anticipate their moves. Each member has a specific skill set necessary for understanding the killers motivations and triggers. This show was created by Jeff Davis and ran from September 2005 to Feburary 2020. Recently it was announced that Criminal Minds would come back for one more season starting November 24th, 2022. This will feature lots of the old cast members. This season will focus on what the characters up to now and how navigate new cases.

The Cast

Across the 16 season, Criminal Minds has had a revolving cast. However, there is one group that is most widely known. They are Spencer Reid, Emily Prentiss, Derek Morgan, Jennifer Jareau (JJ), Aaron Hotchner, Penelope Garcia and David Rossi. This cast was the longest group and many of them were there from the first episode. Each has specific strengths that make them the perfect group for catching killers. Penelope is the computer whiz, Hotchner is the group leader, Spencer is a genius with an eidetic memory, Derek and Prentis do the most take downs, JJ is the media liaison, and Rossi is the father figure for the team. JJ, Prentiss, Penelope, and Rossi have returned for the newest season.

Unsubs

When the BAU is introduced to the crime they are trying to solve they don't know the perpetrator. They call this perpetrator an unsub which is short for unknown subject. This unsub could have commited any crime from kidnapping, murder, or terrorism. When the team has been called in, part of the crime has already been committed and they show up to the crime scene. Throughout the episode, scenes of the unsub committing more crimes or escaping police are slipped in. This allows the viewer to see the victim and unsub's point of view and helps create tension. Almost all of the episodes end in the unsub being apprehended by the BAU. However, there are a few episodes where the unsub escapes the BAU.
